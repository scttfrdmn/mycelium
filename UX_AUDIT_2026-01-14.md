# UX/Usability Audit: Spore.host Web Presence
**Date:** January 14, 2026
**Auditor:** UX Design Expert Persona
**Overall Grade:** B+ (Good, with room for optimization)

---

## Executive Summary

Spore.host demonstrates strong visual design and clear value proposition, but suffers from **information architecture fragmentation** and **unclear user journeys**. The dark theme is attractive but may limit accessibility. The dashboard functionality is well-executed, though the relationship between marketing site and application is ambiguous.
